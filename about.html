Our Story

Discover the heart behind our organization and the vision that drives us forward.

How We Started

Encouragement by Empowerment was born out of a simple belief: that no one should have to face life's challenges alone. What started as a small group of passionate volunteers has grown into a dedicated non-profit organization.

Over the years, we have seen firsthand how a single act of encouragement, paired with actionable resources, can alter the trajectory of a person's life. We are committed to being that catalyst for change in our community.

Our Mission

To uplift and support individuals and families by providing genuine encouragement, dedicated mentorship, and vital community resources that foster independence and resilience.

Our Vision

We envision a community where every person feels valued, supported, and empowered to overcome obstacles and achieve their fullest potential, free from the cycle of hardship.
